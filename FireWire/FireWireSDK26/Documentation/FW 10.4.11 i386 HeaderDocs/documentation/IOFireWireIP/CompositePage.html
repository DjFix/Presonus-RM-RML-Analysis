kIOFWHWAddr

kIOFWHWAddr is a property of IOFireWireIP objects. It has an OSData value.

#define kIOFWHWAddr "IOFWHWAddr"
Discussion

The kIOFWHWAddr property describes the hardware 16 byte address containing eui64, maxrec, spd & fifo address
